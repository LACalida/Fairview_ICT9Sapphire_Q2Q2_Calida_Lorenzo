About the OBMC Robotics Club


Founded in 2024, the OBMC Robotics Club is a dynamic organization dedicated to empowering students with the skills and knowledge to excel in the fields of robotics, programming, and engineering.


Mission To inspire innovation and teamwork by fostering a hands-on learning environment where students can explore, design, and build robots that solve real-world problems.


Activities - Workshops: Learn programming languages, robotics mechanics, and electronics. - Competitions: Participate in local and international robotics challenges. - Projects: Collaborate on innovative projects to create functional and creative robots. - Tech Talks: Engage with experts in the field to gain insights into the future of robotics.


Who Can Join? The OBMC Robotics Club is open to students of all grades who are curious, passionate, and eager to learn. No prior experience is necessary – just bring your creativity and a willingness to explore!


Why Join? - Develop problem-solving, coding, and engineering skills. - Collaborate with like-minded peers. - Gain exposure to the latest technology and tools. - Build confidence through public presentations and competitions.
